📜 quest_log.txt — MY STORY
I got into web dev around 2022 in high school . I was on a biomath track, then found the web and everything shifted. I chose Computer Science at UWA specifically because of that spark. In early 2025 I discovered Figma and UI/UX design, and making my own interfaces from scratch made me happier than anything else I'd tried. Late 2025 I started learning HTML & CSS ; watching a design come to life in the browser is a feeling I want to chase forever. Now in 2026 I'm learning JavaScript, building a Mining Operations Dashboard as a real-world project alongside my studies, and weaving in AI tools to work smarter. I document the whole messy, exciting journey on my Instagram. ✦
